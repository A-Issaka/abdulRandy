AI is helpful in many ways, even so that people use it daily, here will go over 4 of ways AI is most used daily.

Use Functionality Examples Significance
Navigation Apps Uses real time traffice data to select faster routes and predict arrival times, making commuting more effective Waze, Apple Maps, and Google Maps Helps people travel every day, and creates a better traffic flow.
Personalized Reccomendations Uses a user's past behavior to filter what he, she, or they might be interested in. Netflix, Spotify, and Youtube. Not significant to anyone but the user, but it gives them an overall much more enjoyable experience.
Healthcare Diagnostics Used as an assistant for healthcare professionals to analyze medical images and predicting potential outcomes for the patients situation, leading to more accurate diagnoses and a better outcome. Medical Imaging Analysis, Early Disease Detection, and Multi-modal Data Analysis Can entirely better patients lives by giving them more personalized prescriptions and possibly even early diagnoses of sever diseases.
Smart Home Devices Learns users habits to optimize energy usage/saving and can enhance security, leading to a much more optimized user experience. Home Security Systems, Automation/Routines, and Adaptive Lighting. Can lower users power ussage, make them safer, and automating things users do daily.
