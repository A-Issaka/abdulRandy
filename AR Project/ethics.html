FAIRNESS AND BIAS

Fairness and bias is probably the biggest ethical consideration tere is. AI algorithms can unintentionally become discriminative if they are trained with biased databases, which can lead to unequal outcomes for different demographic groups. To counteract these risks, its extremely important to use a diversity of databases, and a lot of them. This is important for ethnic communities being equal.

When it comes to societal implications regarding AI, theres one hat more pople talk about than any other one, and thats employment. Yes it will take some jobs, but new jobs will be created aswell. Theres not much to be worried about when it comes to AI taking over jobs, since AI will pretty much never replace humans.
