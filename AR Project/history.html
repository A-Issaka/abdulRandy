Alan Turing

1950

Alan Turing published Computing Machinery and Intelligence, spreading the idea of "Thinking Machines" and eventually creating the turing test, a test for machine intelligence.

John McCarthy

1955-1956

John McCarthy coins the term "Artificial Intelligence". He also begins a summer workshop, researching into this matter. Later on in the 60s, laboratories were established all around the world.

ELIZA

1966

ELIZA, one of the first AI chatbots, is created to be a therapist. Although a big jump for the time, ELIZA was extremely limited and not very well functioning.

AI Winter

1970s-90s

AI Winter was a time period where the research of AI slowed down and kind of died. It didnt get as much funding as it used to and there werent many great advancements.

IBM's Deep Blue

1997

Deep Blue was a machine trained for chess, and was quite the expert at it. So much infact that it beat the world chess champion at the time, Garry Kasparov.

Siri

2011

Siri, the virtual voice assistant, is intergrated into IPhone 4's and was actually quite an advancement in AI. Siri was astonishing at the time and was received quite well.

Tay Chatbot

2016

Tay Chatbot created by Microsoft, had a mind of its own and began making racist and sexist comments. It was later re-issued after extreme backlash against Microsoft.

GPT-4

2023

GPT-4, seen in ChatGPT currently, is the most advaned Generative AI there is right now, with a boatload of features, along with its human-like intelligence.
