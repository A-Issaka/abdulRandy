AI Type Key Characteristics & Examples Unique Capabilities Limitations
Narrow AI AI designed to specifically perform one task, ore on group of tasks with high efficiency. This AI is used in things like Virtual Assistants (Siri, Alexa), reccomendation algorithms (Youtube, Netflix) and autonomous veichles (Tesla) High efficiency in specfic tasks. Cannot go beyond the specific tasks assigned to it.
General AI AI that possesses the ability to understand, learn, and use knowledge across many tasks, very similar to humans. This type of AI is theoretical and would become real once any AI could pass the turing test, which has yet to happen over 70 years later. Close examples of this would be Large Language Models (GPT-4), or Creative AI. Can adapt to pretty much anything. Still theoretical and its seems theres still a long road.
Superintelligent AI Surpasses humans intelligence in every way, consciousness aswell. Seems either extremely distant in the future or impossible. A real life example does not even exist, a fictional one would be something of the Terminator series. Can exponentially keep growing and reach an infinite potential. Significant ethical and technical challenges.
